ABOUT CODE

The Centre for Outreach and Digital Education (CODE) was established in 2022 to integrate and coordinate the various outreach and digital education initiatives of IIT Madras. Since its inception, CODE has taken over the various activities that were coordinated by the Centre for Continuing Education, which was established in 1986. CODE coordinates IIT Madras’s non-campus BS and web-enabled MTech programs, connects with schools nationwide through the IITM School Connect initiative, and drives executive education and certification efforts such as NPTEL, GIAN, and NPTEL+. CODE stands at the forefront of IIT Madras’s vision to democratize education, nurturing a global community of learners driven by curiosity, innovation, and purpose.
